Fire Emergency Solutions

House Fire

Steps to take during a house fire:

  • - Evacuate the building immediately and call emergency services. Fire Brigade: 15
  • - Avoid using elevators during evacuation.
  • - If smoke is present, stay low and crawl to safety.
  • - Do not return to the building until authorities declare it safe.

Wildfire

What to do during a wildfire:

  • - Stay updated through local authorities and follow evacuation orders.
  • - Call for emergency assistance if trapped. Fire Brigade: 15
  • - Close windows, vents, and doors to prevent the entry of smoke and embers.
  • - Move to a safe area away from the fire, such as an open field or body of water.

Kitchen Fire

How to handle a kitchen fire:

  • - Turn off the heat source immediately, if safe to do so.
  • - Smother flames with a metal lid or baking soda; do not use water on grease fires.
  • - Call emergency services if the fire cannot be controlled. Fire Brigade: 15
  • - Use a fire extinguisher rated for grease and electrical fires if available.

Electrical Fire

Steps to take during an electrical fire:

  • - Turn off the electricity at the main switch, if possible.
  • - Call emergency services immediately. Fire Brigade: 15
  • - Use a Class C fire extinguisher; never use water.
  • - Evacuate the area if the fire spreads or cannot be contained.

Chemical Fire

Handling chemical fires safely:

  • - Evacuate the area and call emergency services. Fire Brigade: 15
  • - Avoid inhaling fumes; use a cloth to cover your nose and mouth if necessary.
  • - Keep a safe distance as chemical fires can release toxic gases.
  • - Do not attempt to extinguish the fire without proper equipment.
