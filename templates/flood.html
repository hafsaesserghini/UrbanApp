Flood Emergency Solutions

Flash Flood

Steps to take during a flash flood:

  • - Move to higher ground immediately and avoid low-lying areas.
  • - Do not walk or drive through floodwaters; water depth can be deceptive.
  • - Call emergency services if trapped. Morocco Civil Protection: 15
  • - Avoid bridges over fast-moving water as they can collapse.

Urban Flooding

What to do during urban flooding:

  • - Avoid contact with floodwater as it may be contaminated.
  • - Call emergency services for assistance. Morocco Civil Protection: 15
  • - Disconnect electrical appliances to prevent electrocution.
  • - Evacuate to designated shelters if instructed by authorities.

River Flood

Precautions for river flooding:

  • - Monitor weather updates and river levels regularly.
  • - Evacuate early if flooding is expected. Call for help if necessary. Morocco Civil Protection: 15
  • - Do not camp or park near rivers during heavy rains.
  • - Move essential items to higher levels of your home.

Coastal Flooding

What to do in case of coastal flooding:

  • - Move inland or to higher ground as soon as possible.
  • - Follow evacuation orders from local authorities.
  • - Call for assistance if stranded. Morocco Civil Protection: 15
  • - Avoid walking or driving through flooded coastal areas.

Flood Aftermath

Post-flood safety tips:

  • - Avoid returning home until it is declared safe by authorities.
  • - Call for help to report damage or unsafe conditions. Morocco Civil Protection: 15
  • - Avoid drinking tap water until it is confirmed safe.
  • - Dispose of items contaminated by floodwaters to prevent health risks.
