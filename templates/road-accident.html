Road Accident Solutions

Head Injury

Quick steps to handle a head injury during an accident:

  • - Call emergency services immediately.
  • - Keep the person still and avoid moving their neck.
  • - Apply a cold compress to reduce swelling.
  • - If the person loses consciousness, perform CPR and monitor breathing until help arrives.

Broken Bones

Steps to handle broken bones during an accident:

  • - Keep the injured person still and calm.
  • - Do not move the broken limb unless necessary.
  • - Apply a splint if possible to immobilize the limb.
  • - Seek immediate medical attention.

Spinal Injury

How to manage a spinal injury during a road accident:

  • - Do not move the person unless absolutely necessary.
  • - Call for emergency services immediately.
  • - Keep the person still and provide support to their head and neck.
  • - Reassure the person and monitor their breathing while waiting for help.

Severe Bleeding

Steps to handle severe bleeding after a road accident:

  • - Apply direct pressure to the wound using a clean cloth.
  • - If possible, elevate the injured area above heart level.
  • - Continue applying pressure until help arrives.
  • - Seek immediate medical help for significant blood loss.
