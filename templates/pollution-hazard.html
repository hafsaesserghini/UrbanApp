Pollution Hazard Solutions

Air Quality Monitoring

How to monitor air quality during hazardous conditions:

  • - Use air quality apps or websites to check live pollution levels.
  • - Stay indoors when pollution levels are high.
  • - Wear protective masks (N95) to reduce inhalation of harmful particles.

Water Pollution

Steps to manage water pollution:

  • - Avoid direct contact with contaminated water.
  • - Use water filtration systems to purify affected water sources.
  • - Report pollution to local authorities for quick action.

Noise Pollution

How to manage noise pollution:

  • - Reduce exposure to loud noises by using ear protection.
  • - Implement noise barriers in affected urban areas.
  • - Advocate for laws that limit noise levels in residential areas.

Waste Management

Efficient waste management to reduce environmental pollution:

  • - Practice recycling and encourage the community to reduce waste.
  • - Organize clean-up campaigns to address accumulated waste.
  • - Report illegal dumping to local authorities.
