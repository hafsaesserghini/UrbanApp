Power Outage Solutions

Sudden Power Outage

Steps to handle a sudden power outage:

  • - Check if the outage is limited to your home or affects the entire neighborhood.
  • - Turn off and unplug appliances to prevent damage from power surges.
  • - Use flashlights instead of candles to avoid fire hazards.
  • - Report the outage to the utility company. Morocco Electricity Hotline: 080 000 77 77

Long-Term Outage

How to manage a long-term power outage:

  • - Keep refrigerators and freezers closed to maintain food freshness.
  • - Use generators safely by keeping them outdoors and away from windows.
  • - Stay warm by wearing layers of clothing and using blankets.
  • - Call your utility provider for updates. Morocco Electricity Hotline: 080 000 77 77

Electrical Hazards

Precautions during a power outage:

  • - Avoid downed power lines and report them immediately. Morocco Emergency Line: 15
  • - Do not touch electrical equipment with wet hands or while standing in water.
  • - Ensure proper ventilation when using fuel-powered equipment.

Backup Planning

Steps to prepare for power outages:

  • - Invest in a backup generator for critical appliances.
  • - Stock up on batteries, power banks, and flashlights.
  • - Keep a supply of non-perishable food and water.
  • - Know how to manually operate your garage door or security systems.

Power Restoration

After power is restored:

  • - Turn appliances back on gradually to avoid overloading the system.
  • - Check your food for spoilage, especially in the refrigerator and freezer.
  • - Report any partial power issues to your utility provider. Morocco Electricity Hotline: 080 000 77 77
