Water Shortage Solutions

Immediate Water Conservation

Steps for immediate water conservation:

  • - Turn off taps when not in use.
  • - Use water-saving appliances (low-flow faucets, showers, etc.).
  • - Take shorter showers and avoid baths.

Water Rationing

Guidelines for rationing water during shortages:

  • - Prioritize water use for essential needs (drinking, sanitation, etc.).
  • - Implement scheduled water distribution if possible.
  • - Educate communities on sustainable water practices.

Water Reuse

How to implement water reuse during shortages:

  • - Use greywater for irrigation and non-potable uses.
  • - Install water filtration systems for safe reuse.
  • - Avoid reusing water for drinking or cooking unless it’s properly treated.

Water Delivery Services

How to access emergency water delivery services:

  • - Contact local authorities or water distribution companies.
  • - Arrange for deliveries to homes or community centers if needed.
  • - Ensure the water source is safe and properly disinfected.
